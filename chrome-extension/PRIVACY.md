# Privacy Policy - Captur Chrome Extension

**Last Updated:** January 20, 2025

## Overview

Captur ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Data Collection

### What Data We Collect

1. **Selected Text Content**
   - When you explicitly select text and choose "Save with Captur," we collect:
     - The selected text content
     - The URL of the webpage where the text was selected
     - Timestamp of when the selection was made

2. **Extension Settings**
   - User preferences and settings stored locally in your browser
   - No personal identification data is collected

### What Data We DO NOT Collect

- We DO NOT collect or track your browsing history
- We DO NOT collect data from pages you visit unless you explicitly save content
- We DO NOT access your personal information without your explicit action
- We DO NOT sell or share your data with third parties
- We DO NOT use cookies or tracking pixels

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of:

1. **Vocabulary Learning**: Storing your saved expressions in your Captur account
2. **Service Functionality**: Syncing your vocabulary notes across devices when you use the web app
3. **Service Improvement**: Analyzing aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve the extension

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Extension settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- **Server Storage**: Saved vocabulary notes are transmitted securely (HTTPS) to our servers at `captur.academy`
- **Security**: We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data transmission
- **Retention**: Your vocabulary notes are stored indefinitely unless you delete them

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: View all your saved vocabulary notes at [captur.academy](https://www.captur.academy/)
2. **Delete**: Remove any or all of your vocabulary notes at any time
3. **Export**: Export your data in standard formats (coming soon)
4. **Opt-out**: Uninstall the extension at any time to stop all data collection

## Third-Party Services

The extension communicates with:

- **Captur API** (`captur.academy`): For saving and retrieving your vocabulary notes
- No other third-party services are used

## Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Data Disclosure Statement (Chrome Web Store)

As required by the Chrome Web Store:

- **Data Usage**: We collect and use "User-selected text content" and "Page URLs" solely for vocabulary learning functionality
- **Data Handling**: Data is transmitted securely to our servers and stored for the purpose of providing the vocabulary learning service
- **No Sale of Data**: We do not sell user data to third parties
- **Limited Use**: We comply with Chrome Web Store's Limited Use requirements and do not use your data for purposes unrelated to the core functionality of vocabulary learning
