## Detailed Description

Build vocabulary from content you love.

Captur helps ESL students and professionals build vocabulary naturally‚Äîturn your favorite YouTube videos, articles, and blogs into systematic vocabulary learning with AI-powered assistance.

**How It Works:**

1. **Hover-to-Translate - Instant Vocabulary Lookup**
   - Select any word or phrase on any webpage
   - Small teal dot appears at the end of selection
   - Hover over dot ‚Üí instant inline translation appears
   - Example: "opportunities" becomes **opportunities** _(Êú∫‰ºö)_
   - Creates clickable vocabulary button you can save
   - Supports 18 languages: Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Dutch, Polish, Turkish, Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian
   - 30x faster than traditional dictionary lookup

2. **One-Click Flashcard Save**
   - Click vocabulary button (yellow highlighted word + translation)
   - Button turns green with border highlight ‚Üí saved
   - Flashcard includes: word, translation, definition, example sentence, and source URL
   - All flashcards sync to captur.academy
   - Review at captur.academy with your saved vocabulary collection

3. **YouTube Transcript with Synchronized Playback**
   - Click "üìù" button on any YouTube video
   - Side-by-side view: video + synchronized scrolling transcript
   - Transcript highlights current segment as video plays
   - Click any segment to jump to that timestamp in video
   - Auto-scroll keeps pace with video playback
   - Manual scroll mode with "Jump to Current" button
   - Copy entire transcript with one click
   - Page layout adjusts to 50/50 split for optimal viewing

4. **AI-Powered Key Takeaways & Vocabulary Extraction**
   - Hover over teal floating button (üìù) on any webpage to show sticky notes instantly
   - Click teal button to generate new takeaways from page content
   - AI analyzes page content and generates key points
   - Automatically detects page type (X/Twitter post, YouTube video, news article, product page, or general article)
   - Extracts difficult vocabulary with inline translations
   - Displays in clean white card design with 3 organized tabs:
     - **Takeaways**: AI summary with vocabulary translations (lazy-loaded on first click)
     - **Marked**: Categorized sentences (vocabulary, claims, hooks, expressions)
     - **Reflect**: Your personal notes and reflections with note editor
   - Hover over logo button to instantly restore hidden notes without regenerating
   - Minimize/expand notes with one click
   - All content auto-saves and persists per webpage URL

5. **AI Writing Assistant (Double-Shift)**
   - Select text and press Shift key twice quickly
   - Advanced tooltip appears with 5 AI actions:
     - **Rephrase**: Generate alternative phrasings
     - **Translate**: Translate to your selected language
     - **Synonym**: Find synonym options
     - **Simplify**: Simplify complex text
     - **Reply**: Generate smart reply suggestions
   - Click any option to replace original text
   - Works in Google Docs, email composers, text editors, and all editable fields
